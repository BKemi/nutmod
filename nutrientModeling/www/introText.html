Introduction to the Nutrient Modeling Website

Draft, August 2, 2016

This website presents detailed, country-specific information on nutrient availability, affordability and dietary diversity for scenarios to 2050.

It is based on research that will be published in two papers -- one substantive and one technical that includes the code and data. The tentative titles are

  • Global nutrition security: relative threats from climate change and demand growth
  • Modeling nutrition security scenarios: Description of the technical process

The website, and the results, are currently in development, and are likely to vary each time it is visited. Please treat this as work-in-progress and do not share. If you find something that seems wrong or is questionable, please email Gerald Nelson at nelson.gerald.c@gmail.com.

The website has a tabbed interface; one tab for each topic.

The current/planned tabs are

  • Affordability
  • Adequacy
  • Dietary diversity, Shannon Diversity index
  • Dietary diversity, food group share of nutrient availability
  • Meta data for the project, mainly for developers
  • Meta data for the IMPACT inputs, mainly for developers
